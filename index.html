01 · Privacy by design

On-device AI reads your receipts. Nothing else does.

Point the camera at any fuel receipt. Apple Vision pulls the text. Apple’s on-device Foundation Models structure it into volume, price per litre, total, fuel type, and date — even on crumpled or angled paper.

All of it runs on your iPhone’s neural engine. The receipt photo is stored locally — never uploaded, never sent to any server, never seen by anyone but you.

  • Zero cloud round-trips. The OCR and the LLM both run locally.
  • No analytics, no SDKs. No third-party trackers. No accounts.
  • Works offline. Scan a receipt with no signal — it still parses.
02 · Live prices · Premium

The cheapest pump nearby. Right now.

TankIt pulls fuel prices from official government data — no scraped numbers, no “estimated” values, just what the regulators publish. Austria and France are queried directly; Australia (NSW & TAS) is relayed through a small TankIt proxy.

Stations sorted by what you’ll actually pay. One tap opens turn-by-turn navigation in Apple Maps.
